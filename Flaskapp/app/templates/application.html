Usage

This application is designed to aid in metabolic pathway discovery. SimPathFinder utilizes all available information on annotated metabolic pathways from MetaCyc and KEGG to predict a pathway ontology (or type/class). The required input is a list of partially (i.e. two or three digit enzyme annotations, e.g. EC:1.2, EC:1.4.3) or fully (i.e. four digit enzyme annotations e.g. EC:1.2.2.3) annotated enzymes. The output pathway annotation can either be (i) a pathway ontology, (ii) a list of similar pathways from MetaCyc and KEGG databases, or (iii) both. For more information on the results refer to the publication.
